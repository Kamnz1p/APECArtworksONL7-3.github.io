THE ORIGIN OF ART

Every February, our country celebrates National Arts Month, a special time to honor Filipino creativity and talent in dancing, singing, and artwork. Led by the National Commission for Culture and the Arts, this celebration highlights traditional dances like Mazurka Boholana, which shows grace and elegance, along with beautiful songs and creative artworks that reflect our culture and history. Through these activities, students and communities express their talents, appreciate our rich heritage, and keep our traditions alive for future generations.
